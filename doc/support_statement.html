HP Linux Printing Support

As a convenience to our customers who are installing Linux, HP has provided driver solutions for printers supported by Linux.

The HP driver project provides printing support for nearly 1,248printer models, including Deskjet, Officejet, Photosmart, PSC, Business Inkjet and LaserJet. (Note: not all models are currently supported. See Supported Devices for more information.)

This Website provides support for these solutions. In addition to the support information within this site, mailing lists are available.

Phone and Email support is currently not available for HP Linux Printing Solutions.
